Bacon & Egg Breakfast Cups


Ingredients:

  • 6 Bread Rolls
  • 4 Strips of Bacon
  • 6 Eggs
  • Salt/Pepper

Directions:

Preheat oven to 180 degrees

  1. Flatten the rolls with a rolling pin. As flat as possible without breaking them.
  2. Form flattened rolls into muffin tin that has been sprayed with nonstick.
  3. Fill each muffin tin with a few pieces of chopped bacon.
  4. Crack an egg right over the top of each muffin tin.
  5. Sprinkle with fresh cracked salt and pepper.
  6. Bake for 15-17 minutes or until egg whites are fully cooked.
  7. Add a few more pieces of bacon on top during the last 5 minutes of baking.
  8. Ta dah you are done, enjoy!






Nutella Crepe

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla
  • 2 table spoons of sugar
  • 2 table spoons of butter

Directions:

  1. Place all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth, if you dont have a blender instead whisk together in a bowl until smooth
  2. Strain the batter into another bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and place in the refrigerator for at least an hour to allow all the air bubbles to settle.
  3. Heat a small crêpe pan (or nonstick skillet) over medium high heat. Swirl 1 teaspoon of butter in the pan
  4. Add a small amount of batter to the pan–swirl the pan so that the batter evenly and thinly coats the bottom.Cook for about 30 seconds (until lightly browned) and flip.
  5. Cook for another 10-15 seconds and remove to a plate. Continue cooking the rest of the batter, adding additional butter every third crêpe or so.
  6. Place the crêpes on a sheet tray, cover with aluminum foil, and keep warm in a 225 degree F oven until ready to serve.
  7. To serve, spread each crêpe with your nutella topping: Nutella, and then fold in half, then into fourths, dust with powdered sugar and serve warm.
  8. Ta dah you are done, enjoy!




Banana & Strawberry Granola Bowl


Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 4 strawberrys
  • 100g of granola
  • 180g of yoghurt
  • drizzle of maple syrup
  • sprinkle of flaked almonds (optional)

Directions:

  1. Slice the banana into pieces and cut the stawberrys in half
  2. put the yoghurt into a bowl
  3. add the granola over the top
  4. throw in the fruit
  5. drizzle over the maple syrupspinkle over the flaked almonds (if you wish to add them)
  6. Ta dah you are done, enjoy!
